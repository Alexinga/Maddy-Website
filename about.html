About Me

Maddy Mrugal

Yoga Instructor

Hello! I'm Maddy. Thank you for finding me here. I am an English teacher, Spanish tutor, artist, and longtime yogi. I am so excited to connect with you.

More recently, I am stepping into the terrain of teaching yoga. For the past decade the practices of yoga, pranayama, and meditation have helped me through countless obstacles. Yoga as a multi-faceted, living organism continues to improve my life in astounding ways, and I am honored to be able to offer classes and be your guide.

I am so very grateful to have the chance to work with you. Whether you want to attend a yoga class I am but a click away. Thank you and I look forward to getting in touch.
